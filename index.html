About

I am currently a Ph.D. candidate in Decisions and Information Sciences at C. T. Bauer College of Business, University of Houston. My research interests fall in behavioral operations and human-AI interaction. Specifically, I study how algorithm design influences human decision-making. I use methods including lab experiments, field experiments, analytical modeling, and reinforcement learning.
